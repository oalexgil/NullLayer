# Input Guide

Fill the following variables before generating the privacy policy.

## Required Inputs

### company_name
Example:
Acme AI

---

### website_url
Example:
https://acmeai.com

---

### country
Example:
Brazil

---

### business_type
Examples:
- SaaS
- Ecommerce
- Agency
- Marketplace

---

### collects_emails
Values:
- yes
- no

---

### uses_cookies
Values:
- yes
- no

---

### uses_google_analytics
Values:
- yes
- no

---

### uses_ai_tools
Values:
- yes
- no

---

### uses_payment_processors
Values:
- Stripe
- PayPal
- None

---

### third_party_services
Examples:
- Google Analytics
- Stripe
- Meta Pixel
- OpenAI API

---

### target_region
Examples:
- EU
- Brazil
- Global
